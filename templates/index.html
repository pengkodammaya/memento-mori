Of the men and women of Malaysia, this is the manner of their going — not the howling of the war-dead, nor the long-robed heroes of Troy, but the quiet, enormous toll of ordinary lives. Heart. Breath. The slow cell. The sudden road. The invisible army that crossed every sea.

Ini adalah kisah bagaimana penduduk Malaysia pergi — bukan jeritan pahlawan, tetapi karma yang sunyi yang merangkumi kehidupan biasa.

Book XI

The Fates That Claim Us

Takdir yang Menjemput

When he descended, each shade told him the manner of its ending. So too here: the catalogues resolve into seven fates — seven ways the body fails the soul. Hover, and hear a single one speak.

Book IX

The Voyage of Years

Pelayaran Tahun-tahun

Not all who set out reach the same harbour. Some are taken at the dawn of their lives; most, after long voyaging, drop anchor in their seventh or eighth decade. Below, every year of life — and the dark bend where the young are lost.

The Archipelago

The land itself is a scatter of islands and shores. From the gleaming south to the windward east and the great isle of Borneo, each state keeps its own count of the departed.

Book XXIII

The Return

Kepulangan

And at last he came home, alone with what the dead had told him.

The voyage is done. Behind us lies the whole catalogue — heart and breath, the long shadow, the sudden road, the pestilence that crossed the sea. Each number here was a name; each name, a life that loved and was loved, that laboured and rested, that set out from its own Ithaka and made, at last, the only crossing all voyagers make.

Pelayaran tamat. Setiap nombor di sini pernah menjadi nama; setiap nama, sebuah kehidupan yang dicintai.
